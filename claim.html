UIUX Design - Research / Prototyping

Quick and uniqe web

We have a task to create a claiming website in one week. During the ideate phase, our team thought it would be fun to make something special. So, I designed a website with different interactive buttons, creating a layout that we love.

Roles

Researcher / Prototype
I collaborated with back-end engineers to quickly finish this claiming website. So, I drew a simple flow for engineers to build the framework. At the same time, I started to design the prototype.

Flow

This website only has simple tasks: Users can claim the crypto coins from their NFTs. The primary function is to connect to the user's crypto wallet, check your NFT in the wallet, and claim.

Design

During the design process, we want browsers to focus on the coin designed by our art team. During lunchtime, I saw an artwork, Genesis, drawn by Michelangelo. The painting inspired me. To that, the human and the god point the coin to the user's focus on the target.

The other page saluted the movie E.T. Not only do users focus on the page's content, but also the symbolic "Connection" for the crypto coins claiming.

We have two versions of NFTs, so I need to build a function for users to switch the version's page. Then, I was inspired by the Game Boy Memory Cartridge. Both have the same idea that they can change the contents immediately.
