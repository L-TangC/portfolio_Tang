I'm a UI/UX designer currently studying at Savannah College of Art and Design (SCAD). My passion for connecting with people dates back to my childhood, and it's what led me to pursue a career in Interaction Design. As a human, I believe that all behaviors and actions are part of our interactions, not only with other people but also with everything we touch, feel, and see.

As a designer, my primary focus is solving the problems that people encounter in their daily lives. I follow a comprehensive process, which includes problem identification, research, ideation, design, and testing. My ultimate goal is to enhance products for users and witness the joy on their faces when they interact with them.
