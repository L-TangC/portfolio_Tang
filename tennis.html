Tennis Court Reveration Website

UIUX Design - Research / Prototyping

scroll

Concept

Create a tennis court reservation website that lets the general public see available booking slots and their reservation status.
The client's requirements presented during the meeting could have been more specific, so we referred to various reservation websites and identified pain points in other competing products, then optimized accordingly. We noticed that many reservation websites with shorter time slots needed help with readability, making it difficult for users to quickly understand the status and even harder to click or cancel directly. Therefore, particular emphasis was placed on enhancing this aspect during development.

Design

In the main reservation interface, I enlarged and effectively created color contrast for the selected date box, making it straightforward for users to identify their chosen date. I used green as a button color, intuitively understood by the general public, enhancing the call to action. The blue color used after selection does not compete with the green in terms of saturation. Additionally, I faded the cancel button, indirectly reducing the probability of users canceling their bookings, thus increasing user retention.
The pricing is also prominently displayed on the screen, making the information between users and service providers transparent. In other interfaces, users can access more information and details through dropdown menus or pop-out windows, and hiding this information does not affect the layout, even when dealing with large amounts of data.
