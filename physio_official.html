Concept

The company's physical therapy clinic website is maintained and optimized to enhance user experience and improve artistic quality. It not only allows users to get to know our company's services quickly but also provides them with access to medical knowledge related to physical therapy, increasing trust in our company among users.

Roles

The primary responsibilities include:

  • Fixing bugs on the website.
  • Updating storefront information.
  • Optimizing the paths for users to find information.

The company's website is made more high-quality through various effects on static web pages and the development of a responsive web design (RWD). My work includes visual configuration, branch information, explanations of regenerative therapy pages, therapist interfaces, and more. All pages are comprehensively optimized to enhance the user experience.
