Concept

Collaborating with the clinic's therapists for consultations and integrating the prescription process through the in-development app's backend enables therapists and fitness instructors to consolidate and access data. This optimization streamlines the workflow for therapists, allowing for more effective patient prescription management.

Roles

Researcher / Prototype / Coding
During the initial stages of backend design, extensive discussions were held with various departments. Beyond mere visual layout considerations, multiple interviews were conducted with physical therapists and fitness managers. Throughout the production process and later stages, user feedback was gathered through prototype experiences, leading to modifications that align with the context of physical therapists. The design process began with sketching in Adobe XD, which was then coded into static prototype pages.

Interview

Design

The most distinct aspect of interaction design lies in the color scheme, with three prominently featured: deep blue for call-to-action elements, gray for selected and unselected states, and white and gray for the background.
In terms of dynamics, the design primarily leverages Bootstrap for its foundation, while any unsupported functionalities and dynamic elements were researched online and learned through coding to be implemented.
This project gave me a substantial foundation in backend layout design and practical application.
